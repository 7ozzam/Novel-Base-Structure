## Prologue: Shadows in Stone

The ancient city of Alexandria, cradled between the Mediterranean and the Nile, is a metropolis where history whispers through every stone. Here, among the bustling markets and quiet alleyways, lies the enigma of Alex—a name that has become synonymous with fear and mystery.

Alex first appeared under the cloak of night, his presence announced by the chilling whisper of a young woman’s scream echoing in the alleys. The victim was found sprawled on cold stone, her life cut short by an unseen hand. In those early days, the city’s oldest inhabitants could not recall such a crime, leaving only a trail of unanswered questions and a growing sense of dread.

Over the years, Alex's modus operandi evolved, becoming more complex and sophisticated. Each meticulously planned murder left no fingerprints behind but instead a trail of cryptic messages, taunting those who sought to understand his motives. His victims were chosen with a precision that bordered on obsession—linked by their connections to the city’s intellectual community.

Detective Nada Hassan, a seasoned investigator known for her relentless pursuit of justice, has dedicated her career to solving these cases. Her office in the police station is filled with files and evidence, each one more perplexing than the last. Despite her expertise, every step closer to Alex seems to only deepen the mystery.
