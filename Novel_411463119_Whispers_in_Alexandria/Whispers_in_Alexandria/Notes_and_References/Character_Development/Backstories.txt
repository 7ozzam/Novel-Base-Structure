## Prologue: The Shadows of Time

### Alex's Backstory

Alex, known as the "Serial Killer of Egypt," has haunted the city of Alexandria for over a decade. His origins remain shrouded in mystery, but his first appearance was marked by a chilling whisper that reverberated through the bustling streets. The murder, meticulously planned and executed with an eerie precision, left no fingerprints behind but instead a trail of unanswered questions.

Alex's victims were chosen with a precision that bordered on obsession—each one linked to the city’s intellectual community, either as educators, students, or cultural figures. His modus operandi evolved over time, becoming more complex and sophisticated. Each crime scene was meticulously staged, leaving behind cryptic messages that taunted those who sought him out.

Despite his elusive nature, Detective Nada Hassan has dedicated her career to solving these cases. Her office in the police station is filled with files and evidence, each one more perplexing than the last. Nada believes that understanding Alex’s motivations could be the key to finally bringing him to justice. However, every step closer she takes seems to only deepen the mystery.
